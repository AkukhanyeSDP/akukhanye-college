Our Story

Akukhanya — meaning "light" in isiXhosa and isiZulu — was founded to bring quality further education to the Eastern Cape.

We believe every student deserves an education that opens doors — not just in theory, but in the real economy.

From a small training centre to a fully accredited college with 16 programmes across NQF Levels 4 and 5.
